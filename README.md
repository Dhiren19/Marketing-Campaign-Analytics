# Marketing Campaign Analytics Dashboard

**This project is part of my data analytics portfolio where I practice solving business problems using Excel, SQL, and Power BI.**

## Project Overview
This project analyzes the performance of digital marketing campaigns across multiple channels such as Email, SMS, and Push notifications. The goal was to understand which campaigns and customer segments generate the most engagement, conversions, and revenue.

The analysis combines data cleaning, SQL-based exploration, and an interactive Power BI dashboard to present the results.

---

## Dataset
The analysis uses three datasets:

- **Customers** – Contains demographic details such as age, city, and income bracket.
- **Campaigns** – Includes campaign name and marketing channel information.
- **Campaign Interactions** – Tracks user engagement with campaigns including email opens, link clicks, purchases, and purchase amount.

These tables are connected using campaign and customer identifiers to analyze customer behavior and campaign performance.

---

## Tools Used
- **Excel** – Data cleaning and validation  
- **SQL** – Data exploration and metric calculations  
- **Power BI** – Dashboard creation and visualization  

---

## Dashboard Pages

### Executive Overview
Provides a high-level summary of campaign performance including total revenue, total purchases, conversion rate, and marketing funnel metrics.

### Campaign Performance
Compares campaign effectiveness by analyzing revenue, purchases, and conversion rates across different campaigns and marketing channels.

### Customer Insights
Explores how customer demographics such as city, income bracket, and age group influence campaign performance.

---
## Business Questions Solved

- Which marketing channel generates the highest revenue?
- Which campaigns achieve the best conversion rates?
- Which customer segments respond most to marketing campaigns?
- Which cities produce the most revenue from campaigns?
- Which age group converts the most?

---

## Key Insights
- Email campaigns generated the highest total revenue.
- SMS campaigns showed the strongest conversion rates.
- Push notifications produced the highest revenue per interaction.
- The Personal Loan campaign achieved the highest conversion rate.
- High-income customers contributed the largest share of revenue.
- Younger customers showed stronger responsiveness to marketing campaigns.
